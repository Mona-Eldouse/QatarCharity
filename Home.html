About Qatar Chairty

Qatar Charity is one of the largest humanitarians and development organizations in the world, providing life-saving assistance to those hit by conflicts, persecution and natural disasters and creating durable solutions for poverty using sustainable development programs in various fields.

Mission

We implement humanitarian and development programs, mobilize support and resources, and build effective partnerships to provide a decent life for all.

Vision

We envision a world in which all human beings have the right to live in peace and dignity
